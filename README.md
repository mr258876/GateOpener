# GateOpener

A little project to open the dormitory door with arduino.

Pinouts
0 - empty
1 - empty
2 - LCD D7
3 - LCD D6
4 - LCD D5
5 - LCD D4
6 - speaker
7 - empty
8 - keypad 0
9 - keypad 1
10 - motor PWM
11 - LCD E 
12 - LCD RS
13 - LCD backlight+
A0 - door open switch
A1 - keypad 6
A2 - keypad 5
A3 - keypad 4
A4 - keypad 3
A5 - keypad 2
